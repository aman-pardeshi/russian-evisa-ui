Russia e-Visa Processing Time

Once you submit your completed application, a Russia e-Visa is typically issued within four calendar days. The process is straightforward and takes about 20 minutes if you have all the required documents ready. The application does not require you to submit additional documentation like invitation letters or hotel bookings.

e-Visa Validity Period

The Russia e-Visa is valid for 30 days from the date of issuance and allows a stay of up to 8 days within Russia. It is a single-entry visa, and its validity cannot be extended except in specific emergency situations, such as natural disasters or medical emergencies.
