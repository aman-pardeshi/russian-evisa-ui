What is a Russia e-Visa?

A Russia e-Visa is an electronic travel authorization that allows foreign citizens to visit specific regions in Russia, such as Kaliningrad, St. Petersburg, and the Far East, without the need for a traditional visa. It simplifies the process by allowing applicants to complete the entire process online, from submitting necessary documents like passport copies to receiving the visa, eliminating the need for a visit to a Russian embassy or consulate.

Types of Russia e-Visas

Russia offers three main types of e-Visas based on the purpose of your visit:
Tourist e-Visa:
For travelers visiting Russia for tourism purposes. This visa allows you to explore Russia's cultural landmarks, historical sites, and natural beauty.
Business e-Visa:
For those traveling to Russia for business-related activities, such as attending meetings, conferences, or negotiations.
Humanitarian e-Visa:
For individuals involved in cultural, sports, scientific, or technical events, fostering international cooperation in these fields.
Eligibility for Russian e-Visa
Not all countries are eligible for a Russian e-Visa, including the U.S., Canada, and the U.K., due to political or diplomatic reasons. Eligibility depends on your nationality and the specific region of Russia you plan to visit. Some countries may qualify for all three types of e-Visas, while others may be eligible for only one or two.
